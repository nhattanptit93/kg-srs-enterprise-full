# Raw Requirement Document

## Idea
Build a food delivery app for urban areas.

## Target users
Busy urban professionals aged 25-40 who order food 3+ times per week.

## Core features
- Browse restaurants by location and cuisine
- Real-time order tracking with ETA
- Contactless payment (card, wallet, COD)
- Rating and review system

## Constraints
- Must launch on iOS and Android within 3 months
- Support Vietnamese and English
- Handle 10,000 concurrent users at peak

## Out of scope
- Grocery delivery
- Scheduled recurring orders
